Red Zone
Monitoring

Our AI continuously scans for unauthorized entry into restricted areas, ensuring compliance with safety protocols. The system alerts safety officers in real-time, preventing potential accidents.

Key Feature
How it works

Using AI-powered cameras and sensors, the system identifies when an individual enters a red zone. Alerts are triggered immediately, and location data is sent to the control center.

Work at Height
Safety

Accurates AI monitors employees working at heights to ensure they are using proper fall protection equipment. Instant notifications are sent if safety measures are not met.

Key Feature
How it works

AI analyzes the worker’s position, detecting the use of fall protection devices like harnesses. Alerts are sent if the worker is not properly secured.

PPE
Detection

Our solution automatically scans for personal protective equipment (PPE) compliance, ensuring workers are wearing the necessary gear to prevent injuries.

Key Feature
How it works

AI vision systems check for PPE items like helmets, gloves, and safety vests. If missing, the system notifies supervisors instantly.

Alert

AI technology identifies dangerous zones on construction sites, alerting workers and managers if someone is entering a hazardous area.

Key Feature
